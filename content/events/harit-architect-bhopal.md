The HARIT (Holistic Action for Rejuvenating Indigenous Traditions) Architect Conference was held on 7-8 November 2024 at Bhavan Yojana and Vastukala Vidyalaya, Bhopal. A two-day national congress on sustainable architecture, promoting environment-friendly construction based on Indian traditions. Senior RSS Pracharak Shri Manmohan ji Vaidya spoke about balancing materialism and spirituality for environmental responsibility.
