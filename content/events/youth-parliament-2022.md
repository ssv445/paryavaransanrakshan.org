The National Environment Youth Parliament 2022 engaged 180+ universities and 50,000 students at the zero level. 3,362 students advanced to Level One, and 150 represented in the actual Parliament. Organised with PRIDE (Parliamentary Research and Training Institute for Democracies) and Manav Rachna International Institute. The event featured the Paryavaran Chetna: Environment & Sustainability theme.
