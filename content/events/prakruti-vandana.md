Prakruti Vandana (Nature Worship) was a synchronised ceremony held across India in joint efforts with HSSF (Hindu Spiritual and Service Foundation). A special message by Dr. Mohan Bhagat, Sarsanghchalak, RSS, was delivered. The event achieved 11,00,000 registrations (HSSF + PSG combined) and a Twitter reach of 3,00,00,000.
