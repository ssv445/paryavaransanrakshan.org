The first National Student Paryavaran Pratiyogita engaged 6,000+ schools and 1,80,000 students in environmental competitions. This was the beginning of what would become one of PSG's flagship programmes, growing to 1,25,537 institutes and 15,47,901 students across 118 countries by 2025.
