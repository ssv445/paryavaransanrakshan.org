The Paryavaran Prahari (Environmental Sentinel) programme trained and recognised individuals making outstanding contributions to environmental protection. Built on four pillars: Training (online programs), Sankalp Portal (registration), Gamification (courses, tasks, points), and Social Media (awareness). Achieved 3,50,000 registrations in just 12 months.
