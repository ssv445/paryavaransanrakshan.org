---
Following the formal declaration in Gwalior and the Pushkar vichar manthan, PSG convened a regional baithak in Agra on 22 September 2019 to plan organisational rollout across the North and North-East (उत्तर, उत्तर पूर्व) zones. State-level convenors gathered to align on Prant-wise plans for plantation, water and polythene-free campaigns.
