On World Earth Day 2020, PSG launched a nationwide digital competition (#all4earth) with categories including Eco-Bricks, Drawing for Kids, Poster Making for Youth, and Tulasi Gamala Decoration for Women. The response was massive: 15,367 entries from 506 districts across 44 RSS Prants, with 61% female participation. Top districts: Mainpuri UP (861), Aligarh UP (525), Mansa Punjab (525).
