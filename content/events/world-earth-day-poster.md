On World Earth Day 2020, PSG launched a nationwide digital competition (#all4earth) with five categories: Eco-Bricks for all, 5-Star Home for all, Drawing Competition for kids under 14, Drawing Competition for 15–18 year olds, Poster Making for youth 19–25, and Tulasi Gamala Decoration for women. The format was entirely digital — eComp, eVal, eCert.

The response was massive: **15,367 entries** from **506 districts** across **44 RSS Prants**, with **61% female** and 39% male participation. **9 Prants crossed 500 entries**, led by **Brij Prant (2,755)** and **Punjab (1,803)**. Top districts: Mainpuri UP (861), Aligarh UP (525), Mansa Punjab (525), Agra UP (519).
