Setting a new benchmark for eco-friendly religious events, the Gurugram chapter organised a zero-waste Nagar Kirtan Yatra. Volunteers distributed cloth bags to devotees, replaced plastic plates with biodegradable alternatives, and ran a plogging drive along the procession route — collecting and segregating waste as they walked.
