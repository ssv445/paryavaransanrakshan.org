location: 'Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh'

In the Prayagraj division, volunteers from Paryavaran Sanrakshan gathered to worship amla (Indian gooseberry) trees — reaffirming the traditional bond between communities and the natural world. Participants pledged to protect trees in their neighbourhoods and plant new saplings in the coming season.
