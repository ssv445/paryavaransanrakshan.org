Dr. Rajesh, a local physician and Paryavaran Sanrakshan volunteer in Sirsa district, distributed free flower saplings of 86 different varieties to residents. The drive turned homes and public spaces into blooming gardens, proving that environmental action and beauty go hand in hand.
